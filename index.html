Description

Marks: The Paws represent the the Dogs on each space Mission. Each Rocket represents a particular mission on a specific day in a year.

Channels: Based on if a dog survived the mission the color of the Paw changes. Red denotes not survived. Green denotes survived.

Interaction: No interaction implemented.

Source/Inspiration: Film Flowers The concept of displaying the years and the respective films has been borrowed for the Film Flowers example.

Instead to displaying the flowers, I have displayed the rockets. But the difference is for the Film Flowers example
the number of movies per year is constant, whereas in this visualization the number of missions per year
is not fixed and varies according to the data.
